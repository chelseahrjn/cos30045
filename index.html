Exploring Trends in Pharmaceutical Consumption and Antidepressant across OECD Countries

This page contains visualisations related to Pharmaceutical Consumptions, which is drugs used in diabetes, as well as Anti-Depressants. These visualisations are using data from OECD Health Statistics in particular. Both visualisations present the data to get a clearer information rather than just reading the dataset directly.

One of the visualisations is the annual consumption of drugs used in diabetes, compared with numbers of death which are caused by diabetes mellitus. These two variables are going to be compared, in order to see if there is a correlation between pharmaceutical consumption and the deaths itself.

The next visualisation reads into the dataset which defined daily dosage of NOBA-Antidepressants per 1000 inhabitants per day from 2010 to 2019 for various countries. It aims to shed light on antidepressant use in different countries over the course of a decade.
